How to Play

You will be presented with 5 puzzles to solve. Each puzzle contains a password that must be entered on the home page. Once you complete all the puzzles, the game is over. The top scores sorted by shortest amount of time to complete are shown on the leaderboard page.

Your time starts as soon as you click the button below.
